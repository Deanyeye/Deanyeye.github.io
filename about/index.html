current situation

I’m 18 years old and going to enjoy a wonderful college(SCNU) life soon!

I will major in Computer Science and Technology which I’m really interested in. To better learn it, I build a personal blog and write some articles logging my perception and growth in the future.

In the summer vacation, I have learned part of C Programming Language by watching CS50 in YouTube. My field of interest are Back-end Development and Machine Learning.

It’s a lucky thing for me to study what I have passion in. Just keep going and there’s more to come!

personality

According to MBTI, my personality type is INTP. People who score as an INTP personality type are often described as a logician, and it seems suitable for me. But I believe that I am low-level because the majority of INTP is intellectual and I’m obviously not.

I’m actually reserved but I’m willing to encounter various people. You can learn it through the case that My good friends always say I’m “crazy” and funny.

Moreover, I’m responsible and hard-working so people usually trust and help me. I’m also literally appreciated to my parents, teachers and friends .
